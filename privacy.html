Effective date: May 31, 2026

Plain-language overview: ZuluPress is in pre-launch developer testing. We aim to collect only what is needed to operate, secure, test, and improve our services. We do not sell personal information. We do not post to connected accounts without user-directed authorization.

This Privacy Policy explains how ZuluPress LLC, referred to as 'ZuluPress,' 'we,' 'us,' or 'our,' collects, uses, discloses, retains, and protects personal information in connection with our websites, software, APIs, developer tools, content workflows, documentation, and related services.

This Policy applies to ZuluPress pre-launch and developer-test services. Product-specific privacy notices may apply to future production services and will control if they conflict with this Policy.

Depending on how you use the services, we may collect the following categories of information:

Unless a future product-specific notice expressly states otherwise, ZuluPress pre-launch services are not intended to collect protected health information, payment-card data, broker-dealer credentials, bank credentials, government identification numbers, precise geolocation, children’s data, biometric identifiers, or sensitive personal information beyond what is necessary for user-authorized developer testing.

You should not submit secrets, credentials, tokens, regulated data, or sensitive personal information through public repositories, public issue trackers, screenshots, chat logs, or unsecured channels.

4. Sources of Information

We may collect information directly from you, automatically through your use of the services, from devices and browsers, from third-party services you authorize, from service providers, and from publicly available sources where permitted by law.

We use information to:

  • Provide, operate, maintain, secure, and improve the services.
  • Authenticate users and administer accounts.
  • Enable user-authorized third-party integrations.
  • Process, prepare, transmit, or publish content at your direction.
  • Query creator information, platform permissions, privacy options, status, and limits where required by third-party APIs.

  • Comply with legal, regulatory, security, audit, and platform-policy obligations.
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and protect rights, safety, and property.

6. TikTok Integration

If you connect TikTok, ZuluPress uses TikTok’s official developer tools and APIs as authorized by you. We may process TikTok account identifiers, basic profile or creator information, OAuth scopes, access tokens, refresh tokens, posting permissions, privacy-level options, interaction settings, upload responses, and post status information.

ZuluPress does not post to TikTok without user-directed authorization. You may revoke access through your TikTok account settings or other controls TikTok provides. If you revoke access, ZuluPress may be unable to continue providing TikTok-related functionality.
